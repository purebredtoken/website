About Purebred Token

About

Purebred Token was made by crypto enthusiasts for crypto enthusiasts. By combining some of the best tokenomics from the most successful projects we have released a truely unstoppable token. With Purebred Token (PBT) you can earn passive ETH rewards just by holding the token in your wallet.

By using a simple redistribution/reflection function we are able to evenly spred trading and transaction rewards to holders. Depending on the trading volume and activity rewards can come as fast as every 5 minutes.

Additionally with every transaction 1.5% will be allocated and donated to select charities to rescue dogs and save them from being killed or eaten for food around the world.
